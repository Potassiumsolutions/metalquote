Instant Metal Part Quote

Laser-cut metal parts, priced in seconds.

How to use it

  1. Add your part. Drop or browse a DXF or SVG file. No file? Just type the width and height.
  2. Pick material & thickness from the drop-downs.
  3. Set the quantity. Leave Nesting on for the best price — it packs your parts onto the sheet to save material.
  4. Click Get Quote. You'll see the price, a cost breakdown, and how your parts lay out on a sheet.
  5. Save a copy. Use Save / Print PDF or Save as JPG to keep or send the quote.

Prices are estimates — the final quote is confirmed after we review your file.

Setup & handling · internal only

Charged per sheet by size: $5 per 12×12, $10 per 24×24. Covers unwrapping the sheet, loading it in the machine, hitting start, then removing and packaging the finished parts once the run is done.
